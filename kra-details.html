Tax Registration and Tax PIN

The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) provides a range of online services for Kenyan citizens and residents, including applying for a Tax PIN, registering businesses for tax purposes, and filing tax returns. Having a Tax PIN is essential for anyone engaging in business or employment in Kenya.

The Tax PIN is a requirement for individuals and companies applying for services such as opening a bank account, applying for loans, or registering a business.

We offer a simplified application process for obtaining your Tax PIN and registration with the KRA. By submitting your application, we will guide you through the necessary steps for obtaining these crucial documents.

Why Apply for a Tax PIN?

  • Essential for applying for loans, credit cards, and mortgages.
  • Helps in the compliance of tax laws for individuals and businesses.
  • Required for filing tax returns and receiving government benefits.
