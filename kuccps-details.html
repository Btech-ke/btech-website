KUCCPS Application Requirements

1. Minimum Academic Qualifications

  • Degree Courses: Minimum grade of C+ in KCSE.
  • Diploma Courses: Minimum grade of C- (some require C plain).
  • Craft/Certificate Courses: Minimum grade of D in KCSE.
  • Artisan Courses: Minimum grade of E in KCSE.

2. Additional Requirements for Online Application

  • KCSE Index Number (e.g., 12345678901/2023)
  • KCPE Index Number
  • Birth Certificate Number
  • Valid ID Card or Waiting Card (if available)
  • Parent/Guardian Phone Number
  • Correct Institution and Course Codes
  • Sufficient funds in your M-Pesa for application payment

3. Important Notes

  • Confirm your eligibility for your selected course before applying.
  • Double-check all information entered to avoid mistakes.
  • Remember, KUCCPS allows only one application per window — apply carefully!
  • All applications are done online — no manual or physical submissions allowed.
